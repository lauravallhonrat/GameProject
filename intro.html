Instructions

Main Goal:

The main goal is to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

Game Flow:

The game starts with 2 dealer cards presented, 1 of them flipped so the player cannot see the final score. The player can choose either ask for a card by clicking Hit! or Stand option that let's the player decide to stop asking cards and let the dealer show his cards to compare the final score.
